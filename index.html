Who we are

Built by engineers who've seen the failures firsthand

We are a team of robotics engineers, simulation researchers, and port operations specialists. Pulsar was born from a simple observation: the terminals most exposed to automation risk are also the ones least equipped to rehearse failure.

Our pipeline turns one photograph into a physics-accurate digital twin in minutes, no LiDAR survey, no proprietary data format. From that twin, operations teams run the scenarios that matter: crane collisions, AGV cascades, power-loss emergencies. Practice the worst before it ever happens on the apron.
